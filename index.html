Papa's Burgeria is a casual game in which you work at a burger shop and are in charge! You must take orders, fry the burgers, and build the burgers! Be a good burger flipper and make Papa Louie proud.

How to Play Papa's Burgeria

Flip the burgers with passion

Papa's Burgeria guides you through the basics of flipping burgers and making a buck. Take your first order and follow the instructions on-screen. You'll start by taking the order and frying up the patty before finalizing the burger with tomatoes, onions, sauces, seeded buns, and other cherished burger ingredients.

Manage your time

As the levels progress, you'll be introduced to new customers with more complex orders. The number of customers will also increase. That means you'll need to balance your responsibilities and learn the timings to get the best scores and tips from your customers.

Buy upgrades

Papa demands that you use the tips to buy restaurant upgrades rather than on your own leisurely activities. You have the option to buy upgrades after each level is completed. The shop features items to help improve customer waiting times (like a television), lamps that keep burgers warm, and many other items to spend your hard-earned cash on.

Snoop on your clientele

Papa keeps a profile on all of his most prized customers. You can look in the customer book to see the detailed preferences of your returning customers.
